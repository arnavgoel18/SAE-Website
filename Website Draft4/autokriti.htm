About Us

Autokriti, North India’s largest automotive workshop, has developed over the years into so much more : a melting pot of automobile experience , a launchpad for upcoming engineers , a showcase of spectacle - where the dreams come alive and every step you take is a step towards new experience,where you can never see it all , but when you do see lasts a lifetime , where friendships are born and memories are made into the soft glow of the bonfires.

It’s a little bubble in space and time of infinite possibilities , the place to be in September/October ; and in short :the best learning 3 days of your life. Our mission is to herald the start of unique mission to share knowledge in India and to give a chance to young guns to break new ground on the automobile engineering . Embrace yourself for another technical extravaganza and join us at SAE NIT KURUKSHETRA!
