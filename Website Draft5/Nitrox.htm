ABOUT  US
TEAM NITROX is a student-run techno-managerial team housed within SAE NIT Kurukshetra Collegiate Club. Having extremely meticulous and knowledgeable members, the Team plays a pivotal role in taking SAE NIT kkr, to such grandeur heights. Each year the team design and manufacture an entirely new off-roader right from the scratch to participate in SAE BAJA series. All the varying departments - Designing, Fabrication, Assembly, Testing and Validation, Marketing, come out with their best possible works, whose amalgamation gives an incredible off road buggy. The team laid it’s foundation back in 2010, taking part in two major events namely – ATVC and BAJA SAE. Passionate to build a buggy, whom no stumbling block could hinder, the Team finally bagged 4th position in ENDURANCE run of BAJA SAE’18. To create more space for learning and set the benchmark higher, the Team took a daring decision to take part in one of the world’s toughest championship - BAJA OREGON (USA). There too, the Team performed exceptionally well. Besides this, the Team works studiously hard throughout the year, unaffected by successes or failures, for they have a large vision to chase.
